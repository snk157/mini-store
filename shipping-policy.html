Shipping Policy

All orders processed within 1–2 business days.

Shipping Destinations

Carriers & Methods

  • International: DHL, FedEx, UPS, etc.

Delivery Times

  • International: 7–21 business days (varies by country).

Shipping Costs

Calculated at checkout by weight and destination. Free domestic shipping on orders over specified threshold.

Tracking

Tracking number emailed once shipped. Check status in your account.

Duties & Taxes

International orders may incur import duties/taxes, paid by recipient.
